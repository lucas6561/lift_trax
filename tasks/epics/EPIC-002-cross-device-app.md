# EPIC-002: Cross-device app

## Goal

Make LiftTrax easy to use from a desktop browser and a phone, then prepare it for multiple users and eventual app distribution.

## Why

The app needs to work where training happens. A phone-friendly workout flow matters immediately, while user ownership, deployment, packaging, and mobile app decisions should be made early enough to avoid painting the project into a corner.

## Success criteria

- Day-to-day logging works well on desktop and phone.
- The app has a clear product architecture for browser, mobile, and distribution.
- User identity and record ownership are designed before shared use.
- Release and update expectations are documented.
